Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Buffalo Chicken Dip using Frank's RedHot Original Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cooked Shredded Chicken
  • 1 8oz package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot Original Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles

Steps

  1. PREHEAT oven to 350Â°F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired, and serve with chips, crackers and/or cut up veggies.
